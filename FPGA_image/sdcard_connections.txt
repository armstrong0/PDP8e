
The micro SD card is plugged into a microSD to SD card adaptor.  The pins of
the adaptor are soldered to a right angle wirewrap pin header.

FPGA module    signal   SD adapter
E12 (sd_CS)    CS       1
C12 (sd_MOSI)  DI       2
B14 (sd_MIS))  D0       7
D12 (sd_SCLK)  SCLK     5

GND            VSS      3
GND            VSS      6
+3.3           VDD      4
               NC       8
			   NC		9
